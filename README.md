## 🎮 The Game

Dash across the dunes at golden hour, grab every **sun-coin**, dodge the cacti, leap over the pits — and reach the flag before the mirage fades. 🌅

🪙 **12 coins** to collect · 🌵 **cacti** to dodge · 🕳️ **pits** to jump · 🏁 **one flag** to reach

---

## 🕹️ Controls

| Action | Keys | Touch |
|:------:|:----:|:-----:|
| 🏃 Move | `←` `→` or `A` `D` | tap the arrow pad |
| ⬆️ Jump | `Space` `↑` `W` | tap the **JUMP** button |
| 🔄 Restart | `R` | — |

Plays on **desktop and mobile** — the on-screen controls appear right on the game.

---

## ✨ What Makes It Special

This isn't a template — every piece was hand-built:

- 🎨 **Hand-drawn art** — the explorer, coins, cacti, and flag are all drawn with Canvas shapes. Zero image files.
- ⚙️ **Custom physics engine** — gravity, momentum, friction, and jump-squash, all written from scratch
- 📦 **Real collision detection** — separate logic for walls, landing on platforms, hazards, and pickups
- 🎥 **Smooth scrolling camera** that follows the explorer across a level **3× wider** than the screen
- 🌥️ **Parallax dunes & drifting clouds** for a sense of depth
- 💥 **Particle bursts & screen-shake** when you grab a coin or hit a cactus — the *juice* that makes it feel alive
- 🌇 **A sunset palette** designed for mood, with a glowing sun and gradient sky
